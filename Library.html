Library Reservations

1Hr Library Reservation

Bordertown reserves the right to cancel any library reservation for any reason. Since this service is intended for groups, we require a minimum of four people per reservation. Reserving for fewer than four people can result in losing the ability to reserve the library in the future.

2Hr Library Reservation

Groups of four (4) or more can reserve the library room for two hours. (Max. Occupancy: 12) Bordertown reserves the right to cancel any library reservation for any reason. Since this service is intended for groups, we require a minimum of four people per reservation. Reserving for fewer than four people can result in losing the ability to reserve the library in the future.
